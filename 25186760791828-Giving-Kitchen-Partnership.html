Giving Kitchen Partnership

  • 17 days ago
  • Updated

Gigpro has partnered with Giving Kitchen (https://thegivingkitchen.org/), an organization that provides emergency assistance to food service workers through financial support and a network of community resources.

 

For more information on Giving Kitchen click this link https://thegivingkitchen.org/, and to apply for assistance tap the three lines at the top right of the home page in the app, then tap Pro Perks and open the Giving Kitchen page.
